Introduction

Paycoin is an experimental new digital currency that enables instant payments to anyone, anywhere in the world. Paycoin uses a proof-of-stake method in order for the Paycoin blockchain network to achieve distributed consensus. Paycoin Core is the name of open source software which enables the use of this currency and everyone can contribute.

What is Paycoin?

Paycoin is a distributed peer-to-peer digital currency that can be transferred instantly and securely between any two people in the world. It's like electronic cash that you can use to pay friends or merchants.

What are Paycoins?

Paycoins are the unit of currency of the Paycoin system. A commonly used shorthand for this is “XPY” to refer to a price or amount (e.g. “100 XPY”). Ultimately, a paycoin is just a number associated with a Paycoin Address.

What is proof-of-stake?

Proof-of-stake is the method by which the Paycoin blockchain network aims to achieve distributed consensus and mint new coin. Users prove ownership of an amount of their Paycoin (their "stake" in the system) and in return receive a reward or mint based on an annual percentage of the stake amount.

Paycoin's proof-of-stake system combines randomization with the concept of "coin age," a probability factor derived from the product of the amount of coins (Coin Weight) times the number of days the coins have been held (Coin Age).
